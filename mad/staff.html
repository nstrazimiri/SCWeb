Welcome

On behalf of the Administration and Faculty, I would like to welcome you to St. Clair College and the School of Business and Information Technology. We are very happy that you have chosen our school for your future educational plans.

In the area of Information Technology we offer a two and three year Computer Systems Technician/Technology – Networking programs, and an advanced diploma in MOBILE APPLICATIONS DEVELOPMENT (MAD), and a new post-graduate two-year offering in Data Analytics for Business. Having IT skills opens a wide variety of doors for those wishing to pursue a career in many different business sectors such as private industry, hospitality, manufacturing, education, healthcare, non-profit and the international market. Our curriculum will provide you with the tools and knowledge that are valuable to any organization. You will have opportunities to work together in diverse teams, gain confidence in making presentations, communicate ideas effectively, solve problems innovatively, learn strategies for time management, and acquire advanced computer skills and more.

If a university degree is included in your career path, St. Clair College has several articulation agreements with excellent universities who give our graduates generous consideration for credits earned.


Congratulations on your decision. Student success is very important to us! I, along with the coordinators, faculty members and administrative team are eager to get to know you and provide any assistance you may need.



Warmest regards,
Jim Marsh, Chair,
Zekelman School of Business and I.T.
